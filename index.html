1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how QRaise ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. QRaise is a QR code generator and scanner that also offers an optional account ("Pro") for tracking codes, a wallet for credits, ads, and in-app purchases.

By using QRaise, you agree to this policy. The core features (creating and scanning QR codes) work without creating an account.

2 Information We Collect

DataWhen & Why
Email addressOnly if you add a recovery email to a Pro account — used for password reset.
Account & organization infoOrganization name, member names, and a hashed password when you create or join a Pro account.
Device identifierA generated device code used to link your device to your account and codes.
QR codes & scansQR codes you create or scan. Personal history is stored locally on your device; tracked codes created in a Pro account are stored on our servers.
PurchasesPurchases are processed by Google Play. We store the resulting credit balance (QR/scan credits, ad-free status).
Advertising identifier & usageCollected by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads (see section 5).

We do not collect your name, phone number, contacts, photos, or location for our own use. Camera and similar permissions are used only on-device to scan QR codes.

3 How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: generating, scanning, and managing QR codes.
  • To operate optional Pro accounts: login, tracked codes, members, analytics, and wallet.
  • To process and deliver in-app purchases and apply credits or ad-free status.
  • To send password-reset codes to your recovery email.
  • To display ads and keep the app free for non-paying users.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.

4 Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted providers. Their handling of data is governed by their own policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — secure backend and database hosting for Pro accounts. Supabase Privacy Policy

5 Advertising

QRaise shows ads through Google AdMob (banner and rewarded ads). AdMob may collect and use a device advertising identifier and limited usage data to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted.

You can opt out of ad personalization in your device settings (Google → Ads), or remove ads entirely by purchasing the Remove Ads option inside the app.

6 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only as needed to run the app:

  • With Google AdMob — the advertising identifier, for ads.
  • With Google Play — for purchase processing.
  • With Supabase — to store your Pro account data securely.
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

7 Retention & Deletion

We keep account data while your account is active. You can delete your account and associated data from within the app's account section. You may also contact us to request deletion. Local data (such as on-device scan history) is removed when you clear it or uninstall the app.

8 Security

  • All network communication uses encryption in transit (HTTPS).
  • Account passwords are stored hashed, never in plain text.
  • Database access is protected by row-level security and server-side controls.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9 Permissions

  • Camera — to scan QR codes (used on-device).
  • Photos / Storage — to pick or save QR images you choose.
  • Internet / Network — for accounts, purchases, and ads.

10 Children's Privacy

QRaise is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
