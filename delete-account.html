Last updated: June 7, 2026

1 How to request deletion

You can permanently delete your QRaise account and its associated data using either method below. Creating an account is optional — the app's core features (creating and scanning QR codes) work without an account.

Option A — In the app

  1. Open QRaise and go to the QRaise Pro section.
  2. Sign in, then open the account Settings.
  3. Choose Delete account and confirm.

Option B — By email

Send a deletion request from the email linked to your account (or include your account code) to:

qraise.support@gmail.com

Please use the subject "Delete my QRaise account". We will verify ownership and complete the deletion within 30 days.

2 What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following from our servers:

DeletedDetails
Account & profileAccount, organization name, members, recovery email, and hashed password.
QR codesAll tracked QR codes you created in the account.
Scan recordsScan logs associated with your account's codes.
WalletCredit balance and wallet transactions.
NotificationsIn-account notifications and requests.

Data stored only on your device (such as local scan history) is removed when you clear it or uninstall the app.

3 What may be retained

We may retain a limited set of records where required by law or for legitimate purposes, for example purchase/transaction records needed for accounting and fraud prevention. Such records are kept only for the period required and are not used to identify you for other purposes.
